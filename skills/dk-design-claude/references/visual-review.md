# Visual review kernel

Read after a material render exists. This is an advisory design review, never a replacement for deterministic gates.

Review in this order:

1. **Task truth** — real content, primary action, and state are understandable without invented claims.
2. **Focal order** — the eye reaches the first, second, and third priorities in the intended sequence.
3. **Composition** — grouping, density, whitespace, alignment, measure, and responsive reflow support the task.
4. **Typography** — roles are distinct, hierarchy is earned, body text is readable, and labels remain stable.
5. **Color and form** — semantic contrast, accent budget, geometry, borders, depth, and icons form one language.
6. **Interaction** — hover, focus, active, disabled, loading, error, success, keyboard, and reduced motion are coherent.
7. **Identity** — the approved signature is present without overwhelming repeated work; removing the logo would not make the surface interchangeable.
8. **Production fidelity** — no clipping, accidental overflow, placeholder copy, fake controls, broken assets, or framework regressions.

Record at most three deltas per pass as `observation → user impact → exact change`. Fix structure before polish. Use a second pass only for remaining material drift. Never emit a single beauty score or claim subjective review is a CI pass.
